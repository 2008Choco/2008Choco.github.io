Here you will find hotlinks to all my resources, their Javadocs and my GitHub profile. If you are a web developer and are capable of making a more professional website, please do not hesitate to contact me on Discord.

  • AlchemicalArrows 3

    Arrows with alchemical properties, each performing thematic in-world effects. From fire to water, life to death, magnetic to explosive, this plugin adds arrows to improve the bow vs. bow combat experience on your server.

  • DragonEggDrop - Revival

    Customizable loot tables, unique death animations and attributable dragon templates to change the Ender Dragon encounter each and every time. This plugin is an active fork of PixelStix's DragonEggDrop.

  • LockSecurity 2

    The easiest to use container protection plugin while being all-the-more RPG-friendly. Lock your chests and doors with keys, prevent them from being destroyed from explosions, fires or players and protect your loot.

  • VeinMiner

    Mining a single ore while holding shift destroys the entire vein while respecting tool enchantments. This plugin aims to recreate the Forge mod, VeinMiner, in an efficient, flexible, customizable and feature-filled way.
